Boston is the next stop of our CTO on the US map!

This city gave the opportunity to visit the largest clean tech startup incubator in the United States - Greentownlabs. It’s a global center that is filled with passionate entrepreneurs solving big energy and environmental problems. All these startups represent the engineers, business experts, physicists, chemists, botanists, roboticists and other industry pioneers who are working day and night to build the next product to address society’s environmental challenges.
Great opportunity to find people holding the same views, as well as to find new business partners. Boston’s events are not over yet as Oleg was invited to The Artisan’s Asylum - non-profit community fabrication center that empowers individuals to give form to their ideas through membership, education, and workspace.
Great time to become a witness of innovation birth!

Hello from Los Angeles!

We have some great news! Our CTO - Oleg Kondrashov and our CFO - Michael Tavis are now in LA. Just recently they have visited "Crash Space", one of the first hackerspaces that were established in Los Angeles. An important strategic meeting with its founders took place where the ways of cooperation have been discussed. The next stop was "La Kretz" - the innovation campus of Laci company. It was opened in October of 2017 and has already drawn a lot of attention. The La Kretz Innovation Campus is Los Angeles’ new cleantech industry hub – a place where entrepreneurs, engineers, scientists and policymakers can collaborate, promote and support the development of clean technologies and LA’s green economy. It was a pleasant meeting with La Kretz's staff and a useful experience for Hackspace Capital.

Working visit to Czech Republic!

Last week the CTO of Hackspace Capital, Oleg Kondrashov, undertook a working visit to Czech Republic. Every day of the trip was filled with various events: many groundbreaking meetings with the influential and pioneering companies and people. Major issues of the microgrant functioning were discussed with the Ambassador of Czech Republic in European Union - Milena Visenova. The representative of Czech Export Bank, - Michael Pretl also took part in the meeting. Another important event that was attended by our CTO was a meeting with the heads of all the departments of Czechinvest – the leading investment and business development agency.
The final stop of the visit was the business meeting with Arnošt Marks - the Adviser of Deputy Prime Minister of Czech Republic. During the meeting there has been a discussing regarding the options of possible cooperation in the field of grant support.
