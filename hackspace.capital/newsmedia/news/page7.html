“Russian MeetUp” in Vladivostok.

On 23-25 of October, a “Russian MeetUp” took place in Vladivostok. It has gathered investors and software/hardware developement companies from around the world.

One of the speakers of this MeetUp was Oleg Kondrashev – the CTO of Hackspace Capital fund and the CEO of EnCata Company. The head of the engineering catalyst has shared the experience regarding his work with hardware projects and told about the plans of development for Hackspace Capital ecosystem.

After the speech, Oleg and a couple of young hardware startups had a discussion regarding the possibility of investments in those projects and their inclusion into the fund’s ecosystem.

Another Dubai Solar, WETEX exibitions and World Green Economy Summit took place in the United Arab Emirates this week. Both parallel exibitions were devoted to "green" technologies, that solve global and regional environmental and economic problems.

EnCata Hardware (IoT&Sci-Tech) meetup

On 26th of September in business-incubator of Minsk high tech park an EnCata Hardware (IoT&Sci-Tech) meetup took place. Dozens of talented specialists and inventors came to gain experience about the successful realization of hardware projects and also to get inspiration for new ideas. Petr Dudin, the CCO of Hackspace Capital Fund and the development manager of EnCata Company, shed light on some new investing trends in the field of hardware and sci-tech and showed the advantages of investment funds.
