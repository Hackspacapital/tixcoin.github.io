Nazarbayev University Tour!

This week our CTO Oleg Kondrashov has visited Nazarbayev University. The main distinctive feature of the university is its innovative approach. This educational facility strives to put together higher education reforms and that way to contribute to the establishment of Astana as an international innovation and knowledge hub.
Oleg Kondrashov was accompanied by Diaz Baiseitov, the head of the Corporate University of the largest construction holding BI Group, and coordinator of the research center BI Lab. Together they made a tour around the university discussing details of possible cooperation. It’s always a big pleasure to meet with such vivid people like Diaz Baiseitov. Diaz is engaged in the platform formation for the development of university startups, and the development of a personal project in the field of renewable energy.
